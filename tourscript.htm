Introduction and Briefing (5 minutes)

●     Sanchya - start timer for 5 minutes.

●     Andrew - start recording screen

●     Metrics Trackers - be logged into penguinators gmail account!

●     Monica - Say “Good evening everyone, we are the Penguinators - Brooks, Jacob, Katrina, Monica, and Sanchya. Tonight, we will be taking you guys on our Club Penguin tour and attempting our 3 tasks.”

●     Share link to [OFFICIAL TOUR DOC].

●     Monica - Say “Before we get any further, can everyone please have our Tour Doc open, and also be logged into Club Penguin? Please log into the server SLUSHY”

○     *note: If Slushy is down, use Flurry as a back-up.

●     Briefly read history of Club Penguin (read from team website home page)

○     Monica - Say “Club Penguin is a game launched in 2005, where players can use penguin avatars in a winter-themed world and do a bunch of activities, like play games, chat with friends, pelt strangers with snowballs, etc. Sadly, it’s closing down on March 29th, so it’s nice that we all get to be gathered here before it goes up in flames.”

●     Briefly go over rules (read from Tour Doc)

○     Monica - Say “Now as for the rules. As you can see in the Tour Doc using the link in the chat, we have 4 big rules. First, don’t throw snowballs at other penguins without their consent. Second, don’t stand directly on or cover up other penguins. Third, don’t use mean language in chat. Fourth, choose appropriate clothing for your penguin - meaning no clothes with insulting words or graphics, etc.”

○     Monica - Say “Now, (random name), could you tell me what happens if one of these rules is broken?”

●     Briefly go over tasks and metrics (read from Tour Doc)

○     Monica - Say “And if you scroll down,

●     Share what the prize is for team that completes all tasks first

○     Monica - Say “The first team to have all of their members complete all 3 tasks first, they will earn 100 grade points”

●     Monica - Say “Before we begin, does anyone have any questions?”

●     Monica - Say “After the 15 minutes is up or when all members of a team complete their tasks, we will meet again at the Stadium in (Server Name). If you finish early, you can get to the Stadium and wait until time runs out. In the meantime, please keep your headphones on in Sococo for time warnings. If you have any questions, feel free to message one of the Penguinators.”

Starting and Doing Tasks (15 minutes)

●     Sanchya - start timer for 15 minutes - also say “10 minute warning, 5 minute warning”, etc.

●     Monica - Say “And now, you can go ahead and get started on the tasks! The 15 minute time starts now!”

●     Everyone → be available for questions throughout the tasks in case anyone gets stuck.

●     ***Metrics Trackers - Track emails of screenshots that come in. “Star” emails that have been counted and communicate to each other about which email you’re verifying!

●     Andrew - film different people working through the tasks

○     a.k.a. Get a wide variety of shots

●     ***When 15 minutes is up:

○     Sanchya - Say “15 minutes is up - it is now (current time). Stop what you’re doing, and please come to the Stadium in the SLUSHY server!” (repeat if needed)

○     ***Metrics Trackers***, don’t count emails that come in after the stop time that Sanchya says.

Ending and Debrief (5 minutes)

●     Sanchya - start timer for 5 min.

●     Everyone - Make sure everyone is in the stadium.

●     Metrics Trackers - finish up tallying emails and finalize the winner

●     Announce winning team and announce their prize.

○     Monica - Say “So now that everyone is here, let’s announce the winning team! The winner is… drumroll please……. (color) team! Congratulations, you guys will each earn 100 grade points!”

●     Conclude tour

○     Monica - Say “And with that, our tour comes to an end! Everyone did a fantastic job, and the Metrics Trackers were very impressed by the variety of screenshots that came in. We hope you all enjoyed your experience in Club Penguin. As for now, I will be passing it onto next team to present.”
