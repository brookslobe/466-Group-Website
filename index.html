Background of Club Penguin

Club Penguin is a MMO (massively multiplayer online) game that was officially launched in October 2005 [1]. The game is primarily targeted towards children and preteens. Players interact with cartoon penguin avatars set in a winter-themed virtual world with various activities, such as playing games, chatting, building homes, and decorating with various accessories [2]. It is one of the most popular virtual worlds used by kids - as of 2013, over 200 million penguins have been created [3]. Unfortunately, Club Penguin is set to shut down on March 29, 2017 as the company is planning to release Club Penguin Island, a new game meant for mobile platforms in the same month [2].
