Instructions for the teams

  1. There is an overall time limit of 15 MINUTES to complete all 3 tasks.
  2. Complete all 3 tasks as quickly as you can! You can complete them in any order.
  3. As you complete each task, take a screenshot according to the metrics above.
  4. As you complete each task, take a screenshot according to the metrics above.
  5. After you have completed all 3 tasks, in a SINGLE email, attach your 3 screenshots and send to: penguinators466@gmail.com
    • In the subject line, format it like: “MONICA RED” → so essentially, your name and your team color
  6. Our Penguinators members will verify screenshots and mark who has completed all 3 tasks
    • If your email does not contain screenshots from all 3 tasks, it will not count as complete!!!
  7. The first team to have all of their members complete all 3 tasks the quickest will receive a prize!
